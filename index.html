About

I'm a software engineer at PT. Sungwoo Indonesia, an automotive seat-belt manufacturer, where I digitalize the shop floor: material receiving, production scheduling, stock tracking, and the KPI dashboards that keep improvement honest. My tools run every shift, used by operators and planners who only care how it works when the line is moving.

That's the constraint I design for. A scanner that fails once at the receiving dock loses trust faster than any feature can win it back, so I care about whether something holds up under pressure.

Outside of work I run trails around East Java, and build side projects — usually the kind my household actually uses.
